PRIVACY BY DESIGN

A small, explicit data boundary.

Computer Warrior is an early private beta. This page explains the product’s current technical privacy boundary in plain language. A production legal privacy policy will be added before public account registration opens.

What the Windows tracker calculates

The tracker converts physical input signals into aggregate counters for keyboard, mouse clicks, cursor distance, and scrolling. It also calculates session, daily, and lifetime XP totals. Daily goals, seven-day history, and focus quests remain local to the PC.

What is stored locally

  • Aggregate XP counters and progress remainders.
  • The current local day and recent aggregate daily totals.
  • The local daily goal and completed focus-quest summaries.
  • A random installation device ID and pending aggregate sync batches when online features are enabled.
  • The account session token in Windows Credential Manager, not in the browser.

What optional account sync sends

When you sign in and sync, the desktop app sends a random device UUID, an ordered sequence number, the batch duration and time, and four aggregate XP values: keyboard, click, cursor, and scroll. The server combines accepted batches into account totals.

What is never recorded by the activity tracker

  • Typed keys, typed text, or key sequences.
  • Cursor coordinates, click locations, or event-by-event input history.
  • Application names, window titles, websites, or browser history.
  • Screenshots, microphone audio, camera video, files, or clipboard content.
  • Injected or synthetic keyboard and mouse events for XP.

Website and product analytics

The public website is prepared to use Cloudflare Web Analytics for aggregate visitor and performance measurements without cookies or cross-site tracking. The backend can record a small allowlist of anonymous product events, such as a successful account creation or accepted XP sync. Product analytics never includes a username, email address, account ID, device ID, session token, IP address, user-agent string, or raw activity.

Visitor analytics is not enabled until a Cloudflare Web Analytics site token is deliberately added during deployment.

Leaderboard visibility

Leaderboard participation is off by default. If a signed-in user opts in, the public leaderboard shows only username, rank, and accepted aggregate XP. Device details and private local progress are not published.

Beta status

Public email and Google account creation, account recovery, newsletter consent, deletion/export tools, and a final legal policy are planned before a public launch. Until those safeguards exist, registration remains invite-only.
